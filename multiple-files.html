Make a flip book

from several image files

If you have a movie (MP4) to convert, use the MP4 page instead.

This page helps artists and animators create hand-flipped movie books (also known as flip books) from animation and video files. These flip books are a great way to share and sell your work. This web page is free for you to use, but if you find this tool helpful,consider making a donation to support its development and maintenance. Thank you!

  1. Use the "Choose Files" button to choose your image files. The should have sequential filenames like image_0001.png, etc.
  2. Choose your left-hand margin in inches. A large margin can help your book flip more easily and ensure that more of your frame is visible while flipping, but you will print fewer frames per PDF sheet.
  3. Choose your frame height in inches. The frame width will be scaled according to the aspect ratio of your video.
  4. Click on the "Generate PDF" button and wait for the processing to complete. A PDF will be automatically downloaded to your default download folder.
