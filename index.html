Make a flip book

from a single MP4 video of your animation

If you have animation still frames to convert, use the multiple PNG file version instead.

This page helps artists and animators make hand-flipped movie books (aka flip books) from animations and videos.These flip books are a great way to share and sell your work. This web page is a free for you to use.

There are two ways to upload your work. You can upload a single video, or you can upload individual image files. After your movie is uploaded, a PDF file is downloaded from your browser. Its up to you to cut and assemble the final book.

  1. Use the "Chose File" button to chose your MP4 file
  2. Chose how many pages (frames) you want to print
  3. Chose your left-hand margin in inches. A large margin can help your book flip more easily, and will ensure that your of your frame will be visible while flipping, but you print fewer frames per PDF sheet.
  4. Choose your frame height in inches. The frame width will be scaled according to the aspect ratio of your video.
  5. Click on the "Generate PDF" button, and wait for the processing to stop. A PDF will be automatically downloaded to your default download folder.
